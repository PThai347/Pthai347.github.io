Conventions:
I love going to conventions.
There is so much to do and so much to see, it is always a great place to meet new people.
Having gone to so many of them I would say that my favorite one would be Pax East hosted in Boston Massachusetts.
The reason is that it is close to and it is about one of my other bigger interest which is videogames.

Videogames

To me videogames is more than just a game. To me it is art.
Much like how movies can be considered works of art so to can videogames.
There are bad videogames but there are also bad movies. Great videogames however can let you experience things you normally would not have.
My top three choice for videogames are:
  1. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. A great game with lots of depth and a rich cast of characters, along side a captivating story.
  2. Halo 3. The greatest shooter game of all time. Great multiplayer, intriguing single player story. A great game to play with freinds
  3. Borderlands 2. This game was the game I spent my highschool years playing. So much replayability. A fun game to lose tack of time to.

Outdoors

Despite the two things listed above I do enjoy the great outdoors
I prefer camping and sitting around the campfire over other activities
To me there is just something about sitting around an open flame. Enojoying food and drinks that calms the soul.
I have found that Acadia National forest is the best place to go camping. Its remote with a great scenice view all around.
